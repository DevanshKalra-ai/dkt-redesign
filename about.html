We are DKT Karigar's — Dee Kay Trade Links. Based in Mundka Industrial Area, Delhi, we manufacture and supply precision cutting tools, diamond saw blades, abrasives, and power tool components to professionals across India.

With a steadfast dedication to crafting top-tier tools, our expertise shines through in every product we create. From diamond saw blades that slice through concrete to armatures that power heavy-duty grinders — everything we make is built to perform.

We don't just sell tools. We understand the demands of the trade. That's why 1000+ professionals across construction, woodworking, and manufacturing trust DKT Karigar's.
